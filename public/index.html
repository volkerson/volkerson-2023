Get to know me

I come from a family that worked over two centuries as boatmen. With my fathers dead this tradition came to an end.

In the early 2000s a friend introduced me to Basic. When I saw my first “Hello World” on the screen I was hooked on programming.

Around the same time I finally got access to the internet and I instantly was interested in how are made. From that moment on I knew that I wanted to design and develop websites.

As a digital focused designer my goal is to find the perfect balance between aesthetics, functionality and accessibility.

My design skills in a nutshell:

  • Creating wireframes and prototypes in Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Designing websites, applications and newsletters from scratch.
  • Working on existing projects, editing and improving them with consistency based on given brand and digital guidelines.
  • Creating mockups and new brand material.

My typical work environment includes Visual Studio Code, Terminal, Github and Chrome with Developer Tools. I've got a preference for Mac, but I'm also proficient with Windows.

I've got Hands-On experience with:

  • modern JavaScript, ES6, GSAP, HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, and TypeScript.
  • designing, building and deploying SPAs/MPAs from scratch in React/Vue 3.
  • building custom themes for WordPress from scratch, using custom post types and ACF.
  • using WordPress as Headless CMS.
  • routing and emails (nodemailer) in node.js/express.js.
  • accessibility, search engine optimisation and page speed optimisation.
